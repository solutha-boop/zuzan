Common questions

Everything you need
to know.

Is ZuZan compliant with SARS requirements?
Yes. ZuZan is updated every tax year with the latest SARS tax tables, rebates and thresholds. All payroll calculations, EMP201 returns and IRP5 certificates are SARS-compliant.
Which banks does ZuZan integrate with?
ZuZan integrates directly with FNB, ABSA, Standard Bank, Nedbank and Capitec. You can also import bank statements manually in CSV format from any SA bank.
Can I try ZuZan before paying?
Yes — all plans include a 14-day free trial with full access. No credit card required. You only pay when you're ready to continue.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data is yours. You can export all your financial records, invoices and reports at any time. After cancellation, your data is kept for 60 days before deletion.
Can my accountant access ZuZan?
Yes. You can invite your accountant or bookkeeper as a user with read-only or full access. All plans support multiple users — up to 20 on the Business plan.
Is my financial data secure?
ZuZan uses bank-grade 256-bit SSL encryption. All data is stored on South African servers and we never share your data with third parties.
